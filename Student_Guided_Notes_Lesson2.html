👤 Section 6 — Case Study: Meet Maya
Patient Case File

"Maya is 12 years old. She was recently diagnosed with Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (ALL)."

📋Lab report shows an abnormally high white blood cell count — lymphoblasts are present in large numbers in the blood
🔬Bone marrow biopsy confirms lymphoblasts in uncontrolled division — normal red blood cell production is being crowded out
🧬Genetic panel reveals a mutation in a tumor suppressor gene that normally regulates lymphoblast development
